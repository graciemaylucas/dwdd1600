How can we help?

Combat Harassment and Toxicity

  1. Esports platforms, organizations, and game developers should enforce strict anti-harassment policies and create reporting systems to protect female players from abuse in online communities. Holding perpetrators accountable can create safer environments.
  2. Esports communities need active moderation that targets toxic behavior, both in-game and on social media platforms. Encouraging positive behavior and celebrating good sportsmanship can also shift community culture.

How can we encourage Organizational Change?
Esports teams and gaming companies should prioritize hiring diverse staff, not just in player positions but in leadership roles. Women in decision-making positions can advocate for policies that create more inclusive environments.
How can we Educate and Shift Cultural Norms?
Offering gender sensitivity training to players, coaches, and fans can help break down stereotypes and reduce discriminatory attitudes. Educating people about the importance of inclusion in esports is key to fostering long-term cultural change.
